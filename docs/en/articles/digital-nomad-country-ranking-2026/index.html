2026 Top 15 Digital Nomad Friendly Countries Ranking

Ranking Methodology

This ranking is based on our open-source database's 12-dimension scoring algorithm, combining:

  • Digital Nomad Score: Weighted composite of cost, internet, safety, visa ease, and more
  • Cost of Living Index: 100 = baseline; lower is cheaper
  • WiFi Speed: Average broadband download speed (Mbps)
  • Safety Index: 0–100; higher is safer
