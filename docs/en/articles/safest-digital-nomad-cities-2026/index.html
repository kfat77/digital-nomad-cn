Safety Index Explained

The city safety index combines crime rates, night safety, property crime, violent crime, and discrimination or harassment levels against foreigners. 100 is the theoretical maximum safety level.

Safe City Recommendations

Female Digital Nomads: Tokyo, Singapore, Copenhagen, and Vienna are top choices for solo female travelers and workers, with excellent night safety.

Family Nomads: Osaka, Singapore, Dubai, and Vienna offer quality healthcare, education, and safe environments for children.

Budget-Conscious: For safety on a budget, Prague, Kraków, Da Nang, and Chiang Mai offer excellent value.

European Safe Havens: Nordic and Eastern European cities (Copenhagen, Oslo, Stockholm, Prague, Warsaw) consistently score high on safety with good English proficiency.
