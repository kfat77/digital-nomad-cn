Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. What We Don't Collect

Digital Nomad Guide is an open-source static website. By default, we do not use cookies, collect personal identifiable information, store IP addresses, or track your cross-site behavior.

2. What We Collect

If you choose to enable the optional analytics feature, we collect the following anonymized data:

  • Page path visited (no personal information)
  • Page title and language setting
  • Screen resolution (for experience optimization)
  • Time zone (for regional traffic analysis)
  • Anonymous session ID (expires within 24 hours)

3. Local Data

We use browser localStorage to store only the following data on your device:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Pages you have browsed (for the "Your Journey" feature, can be cleared anytime)

This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly configure a third-party analytics endpoint in privacy settings.

4. Third-Party Analytics (Optional)

We provide integration with third-party privacy-first analytics tools (such as GoatCounter, Plausible). These tools:

  • Do not use cookies
  • Do not collect personal identifiable information
  • Do not track cross-site behavior
  • Do not sell data to third parties

Third-party analytics is completely disabled by default until you manually enable it in settings.

5. Your Rights

  • Clear local data anytime:
  • Enable browser "Do Not Track" to automatically disable all analytics
  • View data we hold about you: View Your Journey

6. Open Source & Transparency

Our analytics code is fully open source. You can review analytics.js for the exact implementation.
