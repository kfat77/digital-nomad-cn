关于膜蛋白的“迷信” 
• 膜蛋白曾经被认为是结构生物学研究的禁区，直到1985年第一个膜蛋白的高分辨率
结构问世才打破了这种迷信。第一个蛋白质高分辨率结构发表于1960年，第一个
膜蛋白高分辨率结构发表于1985年。整整晚了25年。 
 
• 但传统观念仍然认为只有极特殊的膜蛋白才可以结晶。膜蛋白对于结构生物学家从
禁区变成了雷区，敢于去尝试膜蛋白的结构生物学家仍然少之又少。 
 
• 从1985年到2003年的19年间，新颖膜蛋白结构只有75个，平均每年不到4个新结构，
并且大部分是直接从动植物组织中提取、纯化的内源性蛋白结晶而来，即在生物体
内表达量大、生化性质稳定的膜蛋白。

膜蛋白的功能 
膜蛋白的主要功能： 
• 膜蛋白可作为“载体”而将物质转运进出细胞。 
• 有些膜蛋白是激素或其他化学物质的专一受体，如甲状腺细胞上有接
受来自脑垂体的促甲状腺素的受体。 
• 膜表面还有各种酶，使专一的化学反应能在膜上进行，如内质网膜上
的能催化磷脂的合成等。 
• 细胞的识别功能也决定于膜表面的蛋白质。这些蛋白常常是表面抗原。

几种动态过程 
 

几种动态过程 
Ligand-Gated Channels When the ligand, in this case the neurotransmitter acetylcholine, 
binds to a specific location on the extracellular surface of the channel protein, the pore 
opens to allow select ions through. The ions, in this case, are cations of sodium, calcium, 
and potassium. 
几种动态过程 
 Mechanically Gated Channels When a mechanical change occurs in the surrounding 
tissue, such as pressure or touch, the channel is physically opened. Thermoreceptors work 
on a similar principle. When the local tissue temperature changes, the protein reacts by 
physically opening the channel. 
几种动态过程 
A voltage-gated channel is a channel that responds to changes in the electrical properties 
of the membrane in which it is embedded. Normally, the inner portion of the membrane 
is at a negative voltage. When that voltage becomes less negative, the channel begins to 
allow ions to cross the membrane.  
几种动态过程 
A leakage channel is randomly gated, meaning that it opens and closes at random, hence the 
reference to leaking. There is no actual event that opens the channel; instead, it has an 
intrinsic rate of switching between the open and closed states. Leakage channels contribute to 
the resting transmembrane voltage of the excitable membrane.

The structural form adopted by each amino-acid residue: 
• H: helix ( alpha ) 
• E: extended ( beta strand ) 
• T: turn ( often Proline ) 
• C: coil ( random, unstructured ) 
Secondary structures of MP 
• Stretches of residues in H conformation are helical SSEs. 
• Stretches of residues in E conformation are beta-strand SSEs. 
• Stretches of residues in C conformation are loops or coil. 
• Turns (T) are isolated residues, usually Proline or Glycine. 
• Other notation (in 3 states): L for all but H,E.

Potential problems 
• We did not test protein 3D prediction servers as many as possible.  
• When we find a suitable model using AlphaFold database, and then we stop 
model the structure using other servers. 
• This modeling project was funded by an enzyme lab of other University and we

Possible questions 
• Q1. Why do not use Alphafold program directly to predict the model ? 
• Answer: we do not have a Linux server with GPU to setup the AlphaFold program. 
Meanwhile, the global sequence identity between our target sequence and the 
template from AlphaFold database is higher than 92%. Therefore, the model using 
the template is very similar to that using AlphaFold program directly. 
 
Possible questions 
• Q2. Are mutations for enzyme improvement widely used ? 
• Answer: Yes. We are studying several enzymes and effective mutations were 
found and several manuscripts were in prepared. More importantly, simple 
mutations can be easily implemented in practice. We can randomly mutate many 
sites of an enzyme and then select effective mutations. Then, computational 
methods can be used to explain the results.

葡萄糖输运蛋白Glut1 
• 葡萄糖是生物最重要、最基本的能量来源，也是人脑和神经系统最主要的供能物质。 
• glucose代谢第一步是进入细胞，但亲水的葡萄糖自身无法穿过细胞膜进入到细胞内发挥
作用，必须依靠转运蛋白来完成。 
• glucose转运蛋白Glut镶嵌于细胞膜上，将glucose从胞外转运到胞内。 
• Glut1 将葡萄糖从血液中输运到红细胞中，并负责供应葡萄糖到大脑和其他器官。 
• 输运沿浓度梯度进行， 是载体介导的被动输运 
• 葡萄糖输运过程是可逆的 
• 血液到细胞 
• 细胞到血液 
• 例：饥饿时，肝细胞合成葡萄糖，输送到血液 
 
 GLUT1几乎存在于人体每一个细胞中 ，是大脑、神经系统、肌肉等组织器官中最重要的葡萄糖
转运蛋白，对于维持人的正常生理功能极为重要。 
 GLUT1功能缺陷将使细胞对葡萄糖吸收不足而导致大脑萎缩 、智力低下、发育迟缓，并会因葡
萄糖不能及时为人体利用消耗而导致血糖浓度异常升高。 
 GLUT1在癌细胞新陈代谢过程中发挥着重要功能。    
         癌细胞需要消耗超量葡萄糖才能维持其生长扩增， 
     GLUT1在细胞中显著过量往往意味着有癌变发生。 
GLUT1蛋白的重要功能 
        一旦确定了GLUT1组成、结构和工作机理，就有可能通过调控它实现葡
萄糖转运的人工干预， 
        既可以增加正常细胞内葡萄糖供应达到治疗相关疾病的目的 ，又可以通
过阻断对癌细胞的葡萄糖供应而抑制癌细胞。
