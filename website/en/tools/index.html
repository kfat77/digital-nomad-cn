📋 Common Requirements

Most digital nomad visas require:

✅ Proof of stable remote income
✅ Monthly income above minimum threshold (typically $1,500–$5,000)
✅ Health insurance coverage
✅ Clean criminal record
✅ Valid passport (usually 6+ months remaining)

Note: Specific policies vary by country. This tool is for reference only.
