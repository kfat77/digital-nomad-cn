2026 Complete Guide: Visa-Free, Visa on Arrival & eVisa Countries for China Passport

Full visa policy overview for all 60 countries: 11 visa-free, 0 visa on arrival, 13 eVisa destinations.

I. Visa-Free Countries (11)

Chinese passport holders can enter the following countries without a visa.

  • 🇹🇭 Thailand
  • 🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates
  • 🇬🇪 Georgia
  • 🇲🇾 Malaysia
  • 🇲🇦 Morocco
  • 🇰🇿 Kazakhstan
  • 🇲🇳 Mongolia
  • 🇶🇦 Qatar
  • 🇦🇲 Armenia
  • 🇳🇵 Nepal
  • 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan

II. Visa on Arrival (0)

Visa can be obtained directly at the airport or border crossing upon arrival.

III. eVisa Countries (13)

Electronic visas can be applied for online before travel — no need to mail your passport.

  • 🇨🇾 Cyprus
  • 🇪🇪 Estonia
  • 🇮🇩 Indonesia
  • 🇭🇷 Croatia
  • 🇻🇳 Vietnam
  • 🇹🇷 Turkey
  • 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka
  • 🇲🇽 Mexico
  • 🇦🇿 Azerbaijan
  • 🇦🇷 Argentina
  • 🇰🇪 Kenya
  • 🇮🇳 India
  • 🇪🇬 Egypt

IV. Travel Recommendations

For Chinese passport holders, visa-free + visa on arrival destinations offer the most convenience. Southeast Asia (Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore) and West Asia (Georgia, Armenia) are top choices.

For eVisa countries like Turkey and India, apply 3–7 days in advance to avoid peak-season delays.
