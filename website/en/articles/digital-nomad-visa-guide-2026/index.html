What is a Digital Nomad Visa?

A Digital Nomad Visa is a long-term residency permit designed for remote workers, allowing them to live in a country while continuing to work for overseas employers or clients. Unlike traditional work visas, digital nomad visas usually do not permit local employment.

Application Requirements

  • Income Proof: Most countries require monthly remote income of $1,500–$5,000
  • Health Insurance: Usually must cover the destination country
  • Clean Criminal Record: Required by almost all programs
  • Valid Passport: Typically must be valid for 6+ months
