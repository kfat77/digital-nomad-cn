2026 Top 20 Digital Nomad Cities Ranking

Data-driven ranking of 122 cities based on multi-dimensional algorithms covering cost, internet, safety, and coworking ecosystem.

Ranking Methodology

This ranking is based on our open-source database's multi-dimensional city scoring algorithm, combining:

  • Digital Nomad Score: Weighted composite of cost, internet, safety, coworking ecosystem, and community activity
  • Cost of Living Index: 100 = baseline; lower is cheaper
  • Average Internet Speed: Average broadband download speed (Mbps)
  • Safety Index: 0–100; higher is safer
