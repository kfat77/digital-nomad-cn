💡 Route Tips
  • Thailand's 60-day visa-free entry covers Bangkok + Chiang Mai with no extra visa needed
  • Malaysia offers 30-day visa-free entry for Chinese citizens, perfect for a seamless transition
  • Indonesia eVisa is valid for 60 days and extendable — Bali is a must for digital nomads
  • Vietnam requires an eVisa in advance, but the extremely low costs offset the visa fee
  • Buy a Southeast Asia air pass for approximately $200–400 for the entire loop
